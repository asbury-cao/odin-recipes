Chicken Adobo

Description:

Most people that try Filipino food for the first time are introduced to adobo. This dish is known for its distinct soy flavor and is very easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 3 whole peppers (around jalapeno spice level
  • 3/4 cup of vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of peppercorn
  • 4 laurel leaf pieces
